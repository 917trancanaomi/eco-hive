Proven Impact: Real Success Stories

Bonapp Romania: 450 Tons of Food Saved

Since launching in November 2021, Bonapp has saved over 450 tons of food with 350,000+ users across Romania. The platform works with 1,200 vendors monthly and has demonstrated that 90% of users become more conscious of food waste, with 48% actively reducing household waste.

Too Good To Go: Global Leader

Operating in 19 countries across North America, Europe, and Australia, Too Good To Go covers 170,000 stores and has saved over 35 million meals globally from landfills. This translates to approximately 88,000 tons of CO₂ emissions saved, with over 25 million users actively participating in waste reduction.

User Behavior Transformation

Research shows that food waste apps create lasting behavioral changes. Users report becoming more mindful of food management (44%), adopting better storage practices (23%), and planning meals more effectively (20%). One-third of users who reduced household waste estimated a reduction of 10-30% in their personal food waste.
